About Me

I started my career as a software engineer, dual-booting my first linux machine at the age of 14, and landed my first tech internship at 16. I have worked as the technical founder of a venture-backed startup, a software engineer at Meta, and a scientist at UC Berkeley pushing the boundaries of neural interfaces. I have broad technical experience in software engineering, web development, DevOps, electrical engineering, biology, statistics, fabrication, and vacuum systems. I most love teaching, mentoring, and management.
